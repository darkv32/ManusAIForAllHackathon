# Matsu Matcha Brand Design Notes

## Key Design Elements from matsumatcha.com

### Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: Deep forest green (#1B5E20 / oklch(0.38 0.10 145)) - used for text, logo, buttons
- **Background**: Warm off-white/cream (#F5F5DC / oklch(0.97 0.02 95)) - not pure white
- **Accent**: Vibrant matcha green for highlights
- **Text**: Dark green instead of black for softer contrast
- **Secondary**: Warm wood tones, natural bamboo colors

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Large, elegant serif/italic display font - dramatic and editorial
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif for readability
- **Japanese Characters**: Integrated naturally with English text
- **Style**: Mix of italic and regular weights for visual interest

### Visual Style
- **Photography**: High-quality product shots with natural lighting
- **Textures**: Wood grain, bamboo, matcha powder textures
- **Layout**: Generous whitespace, asymmetric compositions
- **Cards**: Minimal borders, subtle shadows, clean product presentation
- **Buttons**: Outlined style with green borders, not filled

### Brand Personality
- Premium, artisanal, authentic Japanese
- Calm, mindful, sophisticated
- Natural, organic aesthetic
- High attention to detail

### Key Differences from Current Dashboard
1. Replace generic blue with deep matcha green
2. Use cream/warm white instead of pure white
3. Add more elegant serif typography for headings
4. Softer, more organic shadows
5. Green text instead of black for softer contrast
6. More refined, less "corporate dashboard" feel
